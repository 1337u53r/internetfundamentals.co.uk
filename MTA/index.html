What is the MTA 98-375 Certification?

The MTA 98-375 is a Microsoft certification aimed at teaching beginners all about HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript. The course consists of ten modules and was designed to give aspiring web developers a basic understanding of web technologies such as HTML, CSS and JavaScript. Students are required to spend at least 4 hours watching the offical Microsoft MVA (Microsoft Virtual Academy) course videos however this is excluding time spent in labs, asssessments and knowledge tests (quizes). This section of the website will provide all necessary information required to sucessfully pass the MTA.
